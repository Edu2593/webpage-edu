Introduction

Unlike the task of week 5, this week I will have to design a circuit and obtain the files to machine it. I have started learning and using the EAGLE software (Easily Applicable Graphical Layout Editor). The code for the machining of the piece was obtained with the help of fabmodules. And, the machining I did on the Roland SRM-20. I tried to program the circuit with USBtinyISP through the Arduino IDE, but I could not do it, for now.
