Final project

Final project:

Electric Tadpole

My final project is to make an electric vehicle to play. I got the idea after watching the PlyFly Go-Kart video from the company The Flatworks.

Watch The Video

Concept

I am sure that, as the main idea of the project, I want to make an electric vehicle. So far, I've had two ideas. The first one, an electric go-kart. And the second, an electric tadpole.

Idea concept

Design

The vehicle will be manufactured mostly in wood. It will use a DC electric motor, and through a display could be possible to see data such as the speed of the vehicle.
