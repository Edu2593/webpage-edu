Computer-Controlled Cutting

Characterize the lasercutter & Design, lasercut, and document a parametric press-fit construction kit

Introduction

It is the first time I make a laser cut on my own. Generally, it is a service, however now I am the maker. Similarly, I had never heard of vinyl cutting before, but here we go. This time, as part of the assessment, there is a group and an individual part. I realized that the group part, which is about laser cutting, is important because is the start point of the individual part. From the group activity the cutting parameters and the measurement of the kerf will be found depending on the material which will be cut. These data are important to make the cut of the press-fit construction kit.

Raster files use pixels, and each of these contains color information, which makes them dependent on the resolution of the image. That is, a good quality image requires more space. On the other hand, Vector files only use points and lines, therefore, they are not dependent on the resolution. A simple application of this type of file would be a logo. However, Vector can not store much color information, so they are not convenient for digital images.
